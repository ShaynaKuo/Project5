"Crawl Outta Love" ft. Annika Wells

Released in 2017 as part of ILLENIUM’s **"Awake"** album, "Crawl Outta Love" is an emotionally intense track that showcases his signature blend of melodic bass and powerful storytelling. Featuring **Annika Wells**, this song is widely considered one of ILLENIUM’s most heartbreaking yet beautifully crafted pieces.

The lyrics describe the pain of walking away from someone you deeply care about, even when you know it’s the right choice. Annika Wells’ haunting vocals, paired with ILLENIUM’s swelling synths and heart-pounding drops, create a track that resonates deeply with listeners who have faced difficult breakups.

"Crawl Outta Love" has been a staple in ILLENIUM's live performances, often accompanied by breathtaking visuals and emotional crowd reactions. The song embodies the raw emotion that sets ILLENIUM apart from many other electronic artists, making it a fan-favorite and a defining track in his career.
