About ILLENIUM

Wikipedia

ILLENIUM, born Nicholas D. Miller, is an American musician, DJ, and record producer. Known for his unique blend of future bass, melodic dubstep, and emotional lyricism, he has captivated millions worldwide. With his heartfelt music and powerful storytelling, ILLENIUM has become one of the most influential electronic music artists of our time.

His albums, including "Ashes," "Awake," "Ascend," and "Fallen Embers," have pushed the boundaries of EDM, blending elements of rock, pop, and indie with deep, immersive beats.
