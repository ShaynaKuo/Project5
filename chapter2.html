"Takeaway" with The Chainsmokers

"Takeaway" is a stunning collaboration between ILLENIUM and The Chainsmokers, featuring the haunting vocals of Lennon Stella. Released in 2019, this song blends future bass with indie-inspired melodies, making it one of the most unique tracks in ILLENIUM's catalog.

The song explores themes of love, uncertainty, and the fear of losing someone you care about. The production is layered with soft synths, emotional chord progressions, and a dynamic drop that feels both uplifting and melancholic. Stella’s voice adds a dreamy, ethereal quality that enhances the song’s emotional impact.

The music video, set against the stunning backdrop of The Vessel in New York City, adds a visual representation of the song’s emotional push and pull. "Takeaway" quickly became a fan favorite, resonating with audiences who have experienced love that feels fleeting yet unforgettable.
